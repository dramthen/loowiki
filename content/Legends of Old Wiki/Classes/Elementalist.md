<div style="display: flex; align-items: center;">
  <div style="flex: 1;">
    The Elementalist can summon various elemental pets, has the very potent Windlace spell, various nukes, and Ice Block for powerful crowd control.<br><br>
	Extremely low health with high damage output and burst potential until out of mana. Fire Elemental offers high single target damage when placed behind the target with Windlace applied. High AoE damage with Ice Sphere when combined with a Reaver in the party.<br><br>
	The Elementalist can only wear cloth type armor. Wields two-handed staffs.

- **Damage Spells:**
	- Aetherblast
		- Mana: 20~
		- Cooldown: None
		- Effect: A low damage, low mana cost, quick casting Magic Damage spell
	- Firebolt
		- Mana: 30~
		- Cooldown: 16 seconds
		- Effect: Normal damage, mana, and casting speed Fire Damage spell
	- Icebolt
		- Mana: 30~
		- Cooldown: 16 seconds
		- Effect: Normal damage, mana, and casting speed cold damage spell - applies a short duration Snare
	- Lightning Bolt
		- Mana: 35~
		- Cooldown: 8 seconds
		- Effect: Spell with high damage variance, can hit about 50% less or more than Firebolt
	- Embed Ice Shard
		- Mana: 35~
		- Cooldown: None
		- Effect: Applies a cold DoT to the target, the final tick deals a larger amount of damage
	- Ice Sphere
		- Mana: 35~
		- Cooldown: 8 seconds
		- Effect: Sends cold orb to the target location which pulses 4 times in an AoE, relatively efficient even against single targets
- **Support Spells:**
	- Ward
		- Duration: 18 seconds
		- Mana: 15~
		- Cooldown: 180 seconds
		- Effect: Self-only spell that prevents all physical damage for the duration. Elemental damage is unaffected and will still damage the caster
	- Windlace
		- Duration: 30 seconds
		- Mana: 30~
		- Cooldown: None
		- Effect: Applies a powerful attack speed haste to the target
	- Fiery Bulwark
		- Duration: 30 seconds
		- Mana: 30~
		- Cooldown: None
		- Effect: Applies a Magic based Damage Shield to the target, threat generated by this spell is applied to the affected target, scales with level up to 15 damage at level 20
	- Ice Block
		- Duration: 18 second (28 seconds with Ice Elemental)
		- Mana: 35~
		- Cooldown: 3 seconds
		- Freezes the target in place, preventing all actions. Breaks on damage. Cannot be re-cast on a target until they are fully thawed
- **Pet Spells:**
	- Fire Elemental
		- Mana: 35~
		- Effect: a low health, high damage pet with additional fire damage on melee and a built-in damage shield that does not stack with Fiery Bulwark
	- Earth Elemental
		- Mana: 35~
		- Effect: a high health, low damage pet with the ability to snare enemies randomly, can have Fiery Bulwark cast on it which is more potent than the built-in Fire Elementals
	- Ice Elemental 
		- Mana: 35~
		- Effect: a non-combatant pet that increases the duration of Ice Block when cast
	- Elemental Overload
		- Effect: a non-functional spell that will likely be removed in the future
