  <div>
    Rogue is a high single target damage class with multiple poisons and bombs that provide both damage and utility, such as lowering his own aggro, snaring, and stunning enemies. Rogues are adept at crippling their opponent in many ways, besides just stabbing them in the back.<br><br>
    Currently, Rogue is also the only dual-wielding class, offering much faster attacks than other classes in the game with long chains of melee styles being possible, the Rogue also has an innate chance to double attack on every melee strike.<br><br>
    The Rogue is also highly dependent on endurance management and positioning to properly execute melee styles correctly. Able to equip cloth and leather, while using primary and secondary daggers.<br><br>
    Rogue is typically the most difficult to solo with requiring the highest APM to pull off successfully. It is possible, but you may find it more difficult than other classes.
