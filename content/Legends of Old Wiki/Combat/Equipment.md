Equipment in Legends of Old is found from slaying enemies, or created at the [[disenchanting]] table.

Non-named enemies in the game drop random items, where stats are randomized on a piece of equipment, random items are also scaled to the monster level that dropped them. However, named [[Enemies]] can drop named items which have predetermined stats, they can also occasionally drop [[Shards]] which can be used at the [[disenchanting]] table.

Both named and non-named items can rarely have additional modifiers on them (some exclusive to named items), such as increases to specific damage types, mana regen, even [[Item Effects|Arcane Renewal]] which has a chance to return mana based on your Arcane stat when you successfully cast any spell. There are many more to find and experiment with, just remember that elemental damage is specific, so 'Magic' increases strictly only affect 'Magic' damage types and won't for instance increase 'Lightning' or other elemental types.

While all classes can use cloth, some can use leather, others will learn to use Chain at level 8, and Plate at level 12 if able. It should be noted however that only Elementalist can wear armor classified as <a href="./Legends-of-Old-Wiki/Items/Cloth-Armor/Robes/">robes</a>.
