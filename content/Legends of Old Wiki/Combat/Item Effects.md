Items can have special implicit effects and/or "proc" effects.

Currently, [[Equipment|random items]] cannot have any "proc", but <a href="./Legends-of-Old-Wiki/Items/">named items</a> can have anything.

- Special Implicit Effects are as follows:
	- Increased Base Melee Damage
	- Increased Melee Style Damage
	- Increased Magic Damage[^2]
	- Increased Cold Damage[^1][^2]
	- Increased Fire Damage[^1][^2]
	- Reduced Endurance Cost
	- Chance to Avoid Endurance Cost
	- Chance to Avoid Stun
	- Chance to Critically Strike[^1]
	- Mana Regen
	- Spells act as if +1 level higher
	- +1 to Elemental Pet Level

+ Special Proc Effects are as follows:
	- Arcane Renewal
		- Chance on spell cast to restore mana based on Arcane
	- Amelioration
		- Chance when hit to store a small amount of health
	- Anger
		- Chance when hit to retaliate with Fire Damage
	- Stormdrinker
		- Chance on attack to deal Lightning Damage based on Dexterity
	- Magebane
		- Chance on attack to deal Pure Damage

[^1]: Cannot roll on Random Items.

[^2]: Only effects damage specifically of its label. For example. Magic Damage only affects Reaver Lifetaps or the Elementalists Aetherblast, etc.
