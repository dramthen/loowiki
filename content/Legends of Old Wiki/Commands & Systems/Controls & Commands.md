**Controls:**
- Movement: WASD
- Jump: Space
- Sit: V
- Auto Attack: Q
- Assist: F
- Target nearby enemy: TAB
- Target Self/Pet: F1
- Target Party Member: F2-F4
- Pet Attack: CTRL+1
- Pet Return: CTRL+2
- Inventory: I
- Character Sheet: C
- Ability Book: B
- Menu: Escape
- Chat: Enter
- Main Ability Bar: 1-6
- Secondary Ability Bar: Shift+1-6
- Target/Interact: Left Click
- Turn Camera: Hold Left Click
- Turn Character: Hold Right Click

**Currently the following chat commands exist:**
- /destroy - destroys the item currently on the cursor
- /played - shows your character creation time and time played

Saves are located at: %appdata%\Local\Loo_Coop\Saved\SaveGames
