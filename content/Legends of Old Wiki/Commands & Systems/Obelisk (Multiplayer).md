  <div>
    The Obelisk, located in the <a href="./Legends-of-Old-Wiki/World/Zones/Nexus">Nexus</a>, allows players to create and join multiplayer sessions.<br><br>
    Simply click on the obelisk to bring up the multiplayer UI.
  </div>
</div>

<div class="top-align-container">
  <img src="Multiplayer_Menu.png" alt="Multiplayer Menu" style="width:300px;height:250px;">
  <div>
    Under "Create Game" you can create a lobby with or without a password, clicking "Create" will use Steam to open a session for other players to join. The room will be joinable whenever the creator of the game returns or otherwise is located in the Nexus.<br><br>
    Under "Find Game" you can search for specific lobbies, or just search all available by clicking "Search", if a password is required to join the session it can be entered into the lower left. Once ready, click "Join" to attempt to join the multiplayer session
  </div>
</div>

In order to keep the game solo friendly, using the Obelisk and bringing friends along will scale monsters to the amount of players, don't fret however as having a group allows each player more time to perform what they do best, such as an [[Elementalist|elementalist]] being more free to cast spells. This can greatly offset the scaling and you'll likely find you have an easier time than soloing.
