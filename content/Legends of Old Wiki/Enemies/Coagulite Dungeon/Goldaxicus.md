This boss is located up the steps on the second floor of the dungeon, short after ascending the steps, take a right past two Coagulite Arcanists.

Goldaxicus has a large health pool, while a standard warrior mob, he deals high physical damage. He is a solo pull.

This boss is of the Golden Giant genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
