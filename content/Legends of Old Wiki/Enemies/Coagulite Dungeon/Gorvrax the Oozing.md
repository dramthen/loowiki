This boss is located not too far inside of the dungeon, taking all right hand turns will eventually lead you to him.

Gorvrax has a large health pool, while a standard warrior mob, he deals additional disease damage on all attack rounds, and has two other Coagulite Warriors that pull with him. In addition there are many patrols that wander into his room that you must account for.

This boss is of the Coagulite genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
