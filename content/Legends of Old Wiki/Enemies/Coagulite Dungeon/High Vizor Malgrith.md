This boss is located just past [[It That Returned]] in the side room off of the spiral descent.

This boss is a caster and will reposition himself to cast, snaring him so he takes longer to move is a viable strategy. He seems to only cast magic based nukes.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Recursive Memory]]
