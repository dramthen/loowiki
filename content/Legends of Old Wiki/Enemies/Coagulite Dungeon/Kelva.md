This boss is considered the Super Boss of the Coagulite Dungeon.

Located at the deepest point of the dungeon, reaching him requires considerable amounts of combat. Kelva is a caster and as a Super Boss is immune to crowd control. Due to being immune to crowd control, many forms of dealing with casters will not be viable as a strategy such as stunning.
