This boss is located in the mushroom caves. It runs through the entire cave system as high speed and will ambush players.

The Black Death will attempt to move behind the target for extra damage and has a poison based DoT that it can proc on its attack. Be ready at all times when exploring the caves as to not get jumped by this spider.

This boss is of the Black Death genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
