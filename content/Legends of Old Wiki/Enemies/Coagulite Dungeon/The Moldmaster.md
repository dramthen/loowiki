This boss is located at the deepest point of the mushroom caves.

Moldmaster is a caster and will initiate the fight by running into the back of the room to spawn a Fungal Creation. Also regularly casts a pointblank area of effect disease damage spell and will apply a damage shield that returns damage to all sources, even damage over time.

It is best to avoid using damage over time and when the damage shield is applied, snare or crowd control the Moldmaster until the damage shield times out.

This boss is of the Mushroom Man genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
