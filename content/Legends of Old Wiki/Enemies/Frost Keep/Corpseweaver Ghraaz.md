This boss is located down the hall past the ruins near the bridge (don't cross over it). There are many Glaciated Corpse three pulls on the way to this boss. There's one four pull of them in the same room, and there are three more around the spider. One of them will patrol around the room and if timed correctly (or Lulled) you can pull Corpseweave Ghraaz alone.

This boss is a caster. If you do not interrupt or overwise overpower him his casts can deal significant damage. Snaring him so he takes longer to reposition is also a reliable strategy.

This boss is of the Frozen Spider genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
