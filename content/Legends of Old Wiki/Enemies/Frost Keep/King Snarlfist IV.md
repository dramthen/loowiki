This boss is located at the very end of the Gnoll wing well past [[Thalnar the Pale]]. Is a solo pull as long as the two groups of four to his sides are cleared first.

This boss is a melee enemy with super boss levels of health and damage output without being classified as one. Likely the most difficult boss in the game at this time.
