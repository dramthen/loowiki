This boss is located at the very end of the Gnoll wing well past [[Thalnar The Pale]]. Is a solo pull as long as the two groups of four to his sides are cleared first.

This boss is a melee enemy with super boss levels of health and damage output without being classified as one. Likely the most difficult boss in the game at this time.

This boss is of the Gnoll King genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Crown of Fangsteel]]
	- [[Crown of the Pale Moon]]
	- [[Warfang Stilleto]]*
	- [[Chillthread Veil]]*
	- [[Executioner's Axe]]*
	- [[Splitfang Hide Cloak]]*
	- [[Ice-walker's Talisman]]*
	- [[Ring of the Frostblood Alpha]]*

*Note: The Gnoll genus originally dropped these items but haven't been reconfirmed as some were moved to the Gnoll King genus. He may or may not drop these.
