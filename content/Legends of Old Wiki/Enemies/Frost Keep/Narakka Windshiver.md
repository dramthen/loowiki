This boss is located in the group of four Gnolls to the left of [[King Snarlfist IV]], well past [[Thalnar The Pale]]. She will social aggro with the rest of her group.

This boss is a melee enemy that deals full cold damage on attack which cannot be mitigated by Absorb or Negation and must be avoided or reduced by Cold Resists.

This boss is of the Gnoll genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
