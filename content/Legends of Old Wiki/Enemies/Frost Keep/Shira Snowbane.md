This boss is located just over the bridge, in the back right room down the stairs all the way back on the left in the opposite direction of [[Thalnar The Pale]]. this boss can be pulled alone, but there is a chance you may aggro a Snow Crawler in the hallway.

This boss is a priest, with high armor and decent damage. It's much more a battle of attrition and may take longer to defeat than other monsters.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Frost-Cracked Bracer]]
	- [[Splitfang Hide Cloak]]
