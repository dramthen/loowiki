This boss is located deep inside the dungeon, over the inside bridge and then straight through, following the path and eventually inside a left-hand room.

This boss is a caster and has two Tormented Spirits out of sight that will social aggro. If you do not interrupt or overwise overpower him his casts can deal significant damage. Snaring him so he takes longer to reposition is also a reliable strategy.

This boss is of the Frozen Haunt genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
