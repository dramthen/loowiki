This boss is located just over the bridge, in the back left room in the opposite direction of [[Thalnar The Pale]]. This boss has two other Gnoll enemies that will social aggro with him.

This boss is a caster. If you do not interrupt or overwise overpower him his casts can deal significant damage. Snaring him so he takes longer to reposition is also a reliable strategy.

This boss is of the Gnoll genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
