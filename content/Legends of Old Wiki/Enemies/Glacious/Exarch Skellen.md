This boss is located in the building directly in front of the ruins down the hall near the bridge (don't cross over it). There are two Glaciated Corpses and a Frozen Legionnaire in the room with him. There's also one Glaciated Corpse that patrols out of the building when you first arrive and if you're too quick may socially aggro the static Glaciated Corpses.

Exarch Skellen is a light tank and will attempt to reposition himself behind the player. He hits very hard and very quickly.
