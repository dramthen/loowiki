This boss is located on the first door to the right after entering the Ravari Fortress.

The boss will social along with the Ravari Maverick standing next to him, it's generally best to kill the Maverick first and crowd control or otherwise deal with Talok second. Talok will repeatedly cast a stun on his target.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Sleeves of Avia]]
	- [[Leggings of Avia]]
	- [[Tunic of Avia]]
	- [[Dagger of Redbeak]]

***Bug: Talok has an additional space as the first character in his name.***
