---
title: Bortak the Cruel
tags: genus/ogre
---
This boss is located in some small ruins and can be found by hugging the right wall from entering the zone. He is a melee-only monster but will likely social with adds if not pulled carefully. He also performed melee styles against the player which greatly increases his DPS.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Greenglow Helm]]
	- [[Ruinous Cleaver]]
	- [[Grubwub Sleeves]]
