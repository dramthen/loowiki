This boss is located just past the [[Sentry Of Eternity]] on a platform with many Ravari deep into the zone. He is a regular melee enemy with no special abilities but does have two Ravari Guard casters outside of his room that must be pulled with him.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Boots of Woe]]
