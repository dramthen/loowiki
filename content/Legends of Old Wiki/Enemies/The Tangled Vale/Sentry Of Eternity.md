tags:

This boss is located deep into the jungle - after passing through the midway ruins, turn right and you'll see him standing on top of a platform.

This boss hits for 250+ and seems impossible to kill. When aggro'd he will state the following: "You trespass where none may walk. Be cast from this plane, and take the lesson with you -- that growth follows humility."
