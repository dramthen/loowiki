This boss is located a bit past [[Bortak the Cruel]] while still hugging the right wall.

This boss has a few Shriller Nestguards near her that you'll need to be careful of when attacking her. Otherwise, the Matron is very tanky but deals a relatively low amount of damage.

	- Nothing
