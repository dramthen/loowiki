This boss is located deep into the jungle - after passing through the midway ruins, heading straight through to the open ruins in the back of the forest with Shadowed Men and Spectral Revenants. He wanders the ruins and can be difficult to spot - carries a mace instead of a sword.

This boss is a regular melee mob with no special abilities, a fairly easy kill.

	-  [[Shadowed Shield]]
