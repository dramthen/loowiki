This boss is located in an extremely well hidden cave. You can locate it by hugging the left wall from the entrance of the zone. The cave entrance is very thin and covered in foliage. Looking out from it you can see the broken elevator and platform just ahead.

There is a single Mudman at the start of the cave which then goes back outside over a thin bridge - it is also possible to run up to the wall to the bridge instead of going through the cave entrance.

From here, you will find many Rotbringer Spiders, it's virtually impossible to single pull them all, but you can thin them out before taking on the larger group. You'll find the Widow Queen just below.

The Widow Queen is a caster and uses Lightning based spells.

- Known Drops:
	- Nothing
