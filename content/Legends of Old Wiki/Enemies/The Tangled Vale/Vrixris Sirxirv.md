This boss is located in the ruins with the Coagulite Wizards outside. He has two Wizards immediately inside the room to the sides of the entrance that are out of sight - these can be pulled first without aggroing him, but be careful of their repositioning as they may run to the boss.

He is a caster that uses Magic, Cold, and Lightning based spells.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Wraps of Latent power]]
	- [[Crud Covered Stud]]
	- [[Staff of the Elements]]
	- [[Arcanist's Keepsake]]
