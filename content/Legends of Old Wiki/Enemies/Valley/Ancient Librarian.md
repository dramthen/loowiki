This boss is located in the first room on the left in the Ravari fortress.

The Ancient Librarian will reposition himself to cast spells. If you do not interrupt or overwise overpower him his casts can deal significant damage. Snaring him so he takes longer to reposition is also a reliable strategy.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Mantle of Forgotten Winds]]
	- [[Wraps of The Last Hour]]
