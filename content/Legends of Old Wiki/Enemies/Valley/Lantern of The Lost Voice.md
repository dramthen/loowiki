This boss is located deep within the newly opened ruined in the caves. Take all right hand turns and you should be able to locate this Corpse Light type enemy.

This boss is a caster type enemy. If you do not interrupt or overwise overpower him his casts can deal significant damage. Snaring him so he takes longer to reposition is also a reliable strategy.

This boss is of the Lantern of the Lost Voice genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
