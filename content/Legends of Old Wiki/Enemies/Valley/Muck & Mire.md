tags:

These bosses are located deep in the cave system of the Valley.

They are socially linked and will pull together, it would be advisable to crowd control one while dealing with the other. They have no special abilities otherwise.

Muck is considered named and will drop items from the drop table, Mire is not considered named and will drop regular loot when killed.
