This boss is located in the cave system very close to Tremor.

This boss can be pulled alone, but must be done so carefully as it may social link other Ravenous Undead, so it may be wise to clear the lesser undead out first before engaging this boss.

Plaguewretch has no special abilities.

This boss is of the Zombie genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
