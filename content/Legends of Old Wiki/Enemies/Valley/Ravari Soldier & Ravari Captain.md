This boss is located wandering the Ravari towers of the Valley.

The Ravari Soldier is not considered named but is the more difficult one to kill of the two. He has less health than the captain but far greater damage - he will also attempt to reposition himself behind the player for greater damage. It is recommended to kill the Soldier first and either crowd control or otherwise deal with the Captain second.
