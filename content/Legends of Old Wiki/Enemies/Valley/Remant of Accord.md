This boss is located in the newly opened ruins deep within the caves, taking a left at the first junction and then walking into the first room on the right with Stone Constructs, he'll be in the back corner. He looks exactly the same as the other Stone Constructs so it's easy to miss him.

This boss is much more difficult than he appears with a very large health pool and high damage output along with disease damage added to every attack.

This boss is of the Remnant of Accord genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Core of Unfinished Duty]]
	- [[Pendant of the Old Keep]]

Boss name is typo'd to "Remant" instead of "Remnant"
