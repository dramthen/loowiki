This boss is located in the newly opened ruins deep within the caves, in a small library like area, nearby [[Varkh the Unquiet]]

This boss is a hexer type enemy. If you do not interrupt or overwise overpower him his casts can deal significant damage and has various debuffs. Snaring him so he takes longer to reposition is also a reliable strategy.

This boss is of the SerErian genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.

- Known Loot:
	- [[Oathbreaker]]
