This boss is located deep in the cave system of the Valley. It may be quicker to access from the rear entrance near the Corpse Light castle.

Although he is a single pull, Tremor hits significantly harder with physical damage than most other monsters - he will also attempt to perform the charge/slam on first engagement that other Golems perform.

This boss is of the Mudman genus and will share its loot table with others of its kind.
