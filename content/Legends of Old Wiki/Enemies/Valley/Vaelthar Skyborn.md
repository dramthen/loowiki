This boss is located on the second door to the right after entering the Ravari Fortress.

The boss will social along with the two Ravari Maverick standing next to him, it's generally best to kill the Mavericks first and crowd control or otherwise deal with Vaelthar second. Vaelthar will repeatedly cast a stun on his target.
