This boss is considered the Super Boss of the Valley.

Located deep within the Ravari Fortress behind multiple false walls. Vareth is a caster and as a Super Boss is immune to crowd control. The Skeletal Centurion is a high damage melee enemy that can be crowd controlled, similar to the [[Ravari Soldier & Ravari Captain|Ravari Soldier]].

Skeletal Centurion is considered a regular enemy and will drop regular loot.

Vareth is a super boss and has an exclusive loot table.

Known Drops:
	- [[Magebane]]
