This boss is located in the watcher tower closest to the back wall.

Since this boss does leap down from its tower, be very careful pulling him as he may social aggro other monsters. Additionally, Watcher Kraeven is a caster and may be more difficult than the average enemy.

- Known Drops:
	- [[Thick Talon Claw]]
	- [[Signet of Valor]]
	- [[Watcher's Skyshard]]
