Shards when used in [[Disenchanting|disenchanting]] will force specific stats based on the shard onto an [[equipment]] piece in addition to the regular stats it would have rolled, this effectively exceeds the power budget for the item allowing you to craft potentially powerful [[equipment]].

Shards drop from most, if not all, named enemies in the game after level 3.

- **Shard Types:**
	- **Other Types:**
		- Power = Intrinsic (Damage on Weapon, Neg/Abs/Eva on Armor)
	- **Resources:**
		- Flow = Mana
		- Vigor = HP
	- **Stats:**
		- Flickering = AGI
		- Brawn = STR
		- Echo = ARC
		- Endure = STA
		- Grace = DEX
		- Insight = INT
	- **Resists:**
		- Spite = Disease Resist
		- Husk = Poison Resist
		- Surge = Lightning Resist
		- Cinder = Fire Resist
		- Veil = Magic Resist
		- ??? = Cold Resist
