title: Patch Notes, July 2nd, 2025
tags:
  - patchNotes
---
- Tremor has gone on a rampage and may have opened up new areas down in the caves he resides in...,

- Added Floating Text Widgets for Parry, Block, and Evade, to better signal when the player has performed these actions. This should help players perform reactionary styles more consistently.,

- Added "Reduced Endurance Cost" and "Chance to Avoid Endurance Cost" mods to the item drop pool.,

- Added support for "Hexer" mobs, which can debuff or snare the player.,

- Added many more Named mobs to fight.,

- Added many new Named Items to be found.,

- Fixed a bug where Multiplicative Scaling from items was not working properly (+20% base melee damage, for example, wasn't actually scaling damage multiplicatively as it should do),

- Fixed a bug where Voltaxic Bomb was applying Fire Bomb's Fire Damage Over Time effect,

- Fixed an issue where ROG items were at a lower power level than intended. Players should notice a significant improvement to mods that roll on ROG items, especially at higher levels.
