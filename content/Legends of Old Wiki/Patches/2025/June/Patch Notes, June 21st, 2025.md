- The crafting bench has been improved in functionality. Previously, it would return a random base type regardless of what you were disenchanting. Now, the items you input have an affect on the outcome. Inputting a majority of weapons will return a weapon, with similar functionality for armor or "accessories". I intend to improve this system further in the future where two swords yields a sword, two bracers yields a bracer, and so on.,

- Updated Slam description to "After a successful block, retaliate with a stunning blow.",

- Updated Luminael's Vengeance description to "From the side, attack the target for elevated damage. Follow up with Luminael's Blessing.".,

- Updated Luminael's Blessing description to "After Luminael's Vengeance, provide a heal to you and allies.",

- Fixed a bug where Luminael's Vengeance was providing a bonus to Evasion.,

- Fixed a bug where Melee Styles were gaining their damage bonus values even on failed styles,

- Added new One Hand Crush options to the drop pool, to help with Paladin itemization.,

- Added new One Hand Axe options to the drop pool, to help with Paladin itemization.,

- Fixed a bug where Longswords were incredibly rare drops.,

- Adjusted Party Difficulty Modifier for 3 and 4 player parties (Mobs will have less HP than they did before the patch),

- Fixed some named mobs loot tables
