title: Patch Notes, June 24th, 2025
tags:
  - patchNotes
---
- Fixed a bug where Festulence's Crypt could not be accessed,

- Fixed a bug where The Moldmaster was not dropping items (he now has a loot table).,

- When creating a new character, the "Help Window" (renamed the Survival Guide) will be shown to the player. Some of the text has been rewritten to be more clear, and hopefully it will help new players understand the games mechanics more quickly.,

- Fixed various miscellaneous bugs that aren't worth enumerating (misplaced objects and the like),

- there was another minor patch early this morning that fixed a memory issue, the game should be much more performant now with long sessions

- Added a new Melee Style to the Reaver called Gore Harvest, which is described as: "Swing your weapon to hit all enemies around you. Each enemy hit heals you for an amount."
