title: Patch Notes, June 26th, 2025
tags:

- The physical damage defense formula has changed. Previously, Absorption did not provide the value displayed due to a diminishing returns curve. The curve was too aggressive, and as such it was not as powerful as intended. Now, Absorption provides the displayed % reduction up to 30%, at which point diminishing returns are applied. Additionally, Negation was far too powerful of a stat, outweighing other options. Negation now has sharper diminishing returns after the first few points.,

- Robes can only be equipped by cloth casters (Elementalists) now. Desirable mods commonly found on robes have been moved to other item slots. This relieves a lot of pressure on the torso slot and makes other slots have more competitive options.,

- Armortypes have had their base defensive stats adjusted based on feedback / analysis of how people are playing. Leather armor now provides Evasion and Negation, Chainmail provides Evasion, Negation, and Absorption, and Plate provides Negation and Absorption. Do note that this does not apply to previously obtained gear and will only apply to gear generated after the patch. _(FloatyNote: The goal for defenses in the game is not that any one defensive layer is overpowered, and that the combination of layers, together, makes for a very powerful character. Being able to Parry, Evade, or Block an attack before it ever comes in, and then reduce damage that actually comes in in a multitude of ways, and then recover from that incoming damage in interesting ways, makes for more meaningful gearing decisions.)_,

- Daggers have had their base damage values increased slightly, and I intend to add more dense itemization options for the rogues weapon slots going forward.,

- Fixed a few issues with mob patrol routes that were leading to insane trains in some zones.,

- Fixed an issue where Sigil of Bulwarking was not being consumed properly,

- Fixed a bug where Gore Harvest was not breaking CC with its AoE cleave effect,

- Fixed a bug where rings/bracers could be put into your mainhand weapon slot,

- Fixed a crash with Gore Harvest
