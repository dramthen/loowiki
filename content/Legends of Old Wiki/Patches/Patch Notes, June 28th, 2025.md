---
title: Patch Notes, June 28th, 2025
tags:
  - patchNotes
---
- Windlace (Haste) has been rebalanced. Its haste value has been reduced, as it previously left little room for Agility to meaningfully impact attack speed. To compensate, Agility now has a significantly stronger effect on attack speed. The fastest attack speeds from before are still achievable when pairing high agility + Windlace, but solo players will no longer be heavily penalized for lacking an Elementalist to cast Windlace on them.,

- Rogues have had their Melee Damage increased significantly. Additionally, Fire Bomb and Voltaxic Bomb have had their damage increased.,

- Added a new "Floating Text Widget" that lets you know when you crit.,

- Added new "+Melee Critical Strike Chance" modifiers that can be found on items.,

- Added new "+Chance to avoid Stuns" modifier that can be found on items.,

- Fixed item generation issues that crashed the game when very high level players killed powerful monsters,

- Did a full audit of every named monster in the game; they should all now have loot.,

- Added many new Named Items to be found, with a focus on slots that were previously too rare or didn't exist in the Named Item drop pool. These new items have been added throughout the entire game, so its worth checking out all the named monsters again.,

- Rebalanced Rogue Melee Style endurance costs so they can perform more abilities in solo play,

- Added a new Rogue Melee Style, "Breaking Point", which is described as: "After performing Eviscerate, stun the target.",

- Added new Reaver Curse spell, "Urtak's Verdict", which is described as: "Curses the target. Tracks damage dealt. When it exceeds a threshold, the curse triggers and deals heavy poison damage".,

- Deployed a minor patch that fixes Urtak's Verdict, which was doing 10x the intended damage : )
