One, unity. A prism--not of
color or light, but of being.
Whole, without division. This is
known.

Then came the Fracture. Its
cause is not yet understood. Its
outcome, clear: multiplicity.
Instability. Drift.

What was whole now moves in
shards--each a fragment of the
original resonance. To walk
among them is to walk the echo
of a great form.

Many seek a cause. This is
natural. But origin is less
important than orientation. You
are here. That is sufficient.

Restore what you can. Observe
what you must.

The Prism is no longer whole.
But neither is it forgotten.
