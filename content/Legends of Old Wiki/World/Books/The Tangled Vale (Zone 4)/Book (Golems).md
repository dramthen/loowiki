This book is found in a cave nearby [[Kroobel'Tan]], it's a difficult to spot cave in the side of a mountain, filled with Mud Golems and Moldy Slimes.

It is said the fire spirits swore an
oath at the foot of the Star-
Forged Anvil: that they would
never burn without purpose. But
after the Fracture, they burned
anyway. Not in anger, nor in joy,
but in hunter. Their covenant
broken, they now wander the
black places beneath the world,
seeking to be bound once more.
