This book is found inside of the building up the side of a mountain past the Ravari Guards

Before speech, before stone, the
world was sung. Er'vwea did not
shape it with hand, but with
breath. And where the breath
passed, the world stirred. The
mountains rose to listen. The sea
gathered to echo. And all that
was made knew its place, until
silence fell and sundered it.
