This book is found in the Grove area by performing a jump to a secondary platform from the tree fort.

She stood unmoved for a
thousand years, carved from
mountain and vow. When the
Prism shattered, she did not fall--
but her eyes began to weep, and
have not ceased. The valley below
is lush now, green with sorrow.
