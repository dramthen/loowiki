This book is found right next to [[Woe]].

In the final days, Er'vwea forged
two relics: one to remember, one
to forget. The Mirror shows the
world as it was before the fall.
The Maw devours what truth
remains. Only the il'jiktar know
which was cast into the sky, and
which into the deep.
