title: Althara

I was a Shieldbearer of the
Radiant Pact--blessed in steel,
tempered in service. I wasn't
anyone special. Just another
oathbound, keeping the flame
where others let it die. We don't
choose glory. We don't seek
command. We serve.

Like all in the Pact, I was bonded
to a cleric. A soulsister. Her name
was Elandra. She had a quiet
strength--faith that didn't waver,
even when mine did.

When the world turned dark, she
was the one who carried the light.
I watched over her sword-arm.
She watched over my soul.

And then... the breaking. I don't
remember pain. Just silence.
The sky folding inward. A seam
tearing through the world--not
wind, not magic, not war, but
something deeper. Like the stars
themselves forgot their names. I
reached for her. She was right
there.

Then she wasn't

When I came to, there was
nothing. No sigils. No chain of
command. No Elandra. Just the
armor on my back and a weight
in my chest where her presence
used to be. But I remember the
vows we spoke beneath the Hall
of Light. I remember her voice,
steady and clear, promising to
walk with me until the end.

So I walk still. For her, if not
with her. And if she yet lives, I'll
find her. And if she doesn't--
then I'll carry her light with me
until mine fades too.
