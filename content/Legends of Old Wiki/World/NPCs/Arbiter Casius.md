Ours is not the only reality, you
know. Since the Fracturing, the
planes have multiplied--
countless realms beyond our
own. From these worlds, mighty
Legends may cross over to aid
you in your journey. You need
only reach out to them.

Indeed. The obelisk aligns the
hidden voids of distant planes,
forming a conduit through which
you may reach those on the far
side of reality.

Simply reach out and attune
yourself to the Obelisk. Its power
will stir, and the veil between
realms will thin. What answers
your call is not for us to decide--
only to witness. Let the currents
of the void reveal who or what
awaits.
