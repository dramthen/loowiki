There you are. Pulled from the
jaws of silence.

And still breathing... That's rare.

The drift doesn't usually let go.
Not without taking something in
trade, that is...

This place... it isn't a beginning.
Don't mistake it for one. It's a
seam--stitched from memory,
ruin, and the pieces we've
managed to salvage. We're not
meant to be here, not truly. But
then, nothing is where it belongs
anymore.

Something elder broke the
lattice. Shattered the weave that
held the worlds close.

Now everything drifts,
untethered, bleeding meeting.

Some call it the Fracturing.
Other whisper older names.
Doesn't matter. The wound is still
open.

You came through it, though.
That means something.

You were caught in the collapse,
same as the rest of us, and still
toy surfaced. The void tried to
take you--almost did. But here
you are. Don't rush to
understand it. That will come.
Or it won't.

Either way, you've work ahead.
Look around. Speak to the
others--we've all lost
something. Some of us more
than others. I'm sure they'll
appreciate a new face. When
you're steady, when you
remember how to move with
purpose... then the path will find
you.
