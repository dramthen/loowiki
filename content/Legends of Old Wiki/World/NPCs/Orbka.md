Orbka come from Orbulg. Way
down the Darahg muck. Big trees.
Big stinks. Big teeth in the water.

Had fire. Had clan. Had meat.
Now? Got none. Got pulled. Like
frog in snare.

One blink, Orbka in swamp. Next
blink... here. Sky wrong. Smell
wrong. Time no move right.
Swamp had rot, but it knew how
to be. This place? Hides it.

Il'jiktar say, 'you fight now. You
guard. You hold line.' Pfah. Orbka
always fight. Not new.

But what line? What edge? What
side is we on? No one say.

Still... Orbka keep blade sharp.
Got nothin' else, Got no Orbulg.
Got no clan. But still got swing.
That mean Orbka still here.

Maybe swamp come back
someday. Maybe not. Still swing.
