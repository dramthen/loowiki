Honestly, I don't know why we're
here. Last thing I remember, I
was havin' a fine stein of mead
with my mates. That's when
everything started to distort into
swirls of madness... I thought I
was losing my mind - Then,
everything began to.. crack.

Yes, crack. It wasn't the building
or the earth that cracked, mind -
but reality itself. Next thing I
knew, it was darkness - for how
long, I have no idea, but I can
remember the feeling. Drifting,
like a ghostly ship, for aeons
Through motionless waters...

You seem to be the newest
recruit to this place. Those
il'Jiktar as they're called have
been snatching people out of the
void. For what? I'm not sure. But I
do know this - I'm happy to be
alive.
